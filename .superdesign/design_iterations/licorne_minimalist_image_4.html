Your Business Setup Journey

A clear, visual timeline of how we transform your business idea into a fully operational UAE company

1

Initial Consultation & Strategy

We analyze your business model, discuss your goals, and recommend the optimal company structure. Our experts review market opportunities and regulatory requirements specific to your industry.

Duration: 1-2 days
2

Documentation Preparation

Our legal team prepares all required documents including memorandum of association, business plan, and licensing applications. We ensure everything meets UAE regulatory standards.

Duration: 3-5 days
3

Government Approvals

We submit applications to relevant authorities, track approval status, and handle any additional requirements. Our government relations team ensures smooth processing.

Duration: 7-15 days
4

License Issuance & Banking

Receive your trade license and establish corporate banking relationships. We facilitate introductions to major UAE banks and assist with account opening procedures.

Duration: 5-7 days
5

Visa Processing & Launch

Process investor and employee visas, complete Emirates ID registration, and provide ongoing compliance support. Your business is now fully operational.

Duration: 7-10 days
